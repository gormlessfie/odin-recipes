Omelette Rice with Ketchup

Omelette Rice with Ketchup 'Omurice' is a popular dish in Japan. It consists of a french omelette over fried rice made with ketchup. The sweetness and tartness of the ketchup provides a refreshing balance of creaminess and acidity.

The fried rice is simple and flexible which allows for many different combination of add-ins such as green beans, ham pieces, or even XO sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of cooked rice
  • 2 Eggs
  • Ketchup
  • Any add-ins for fried rice
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl and mix well
  2. Heat up cooking oil in a non-stick pan and fry the rice for 5 minutes
  3. Add the ketchup and fry for an additional 5 minutes
  4. Add any add-ins, if desired
  5. Set aside the fried rice under foil to keep warm
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and stir while frying until just firm
  7. Combine the omelette and the fried rice and season with salt & pepper
  8. Enjoy!
