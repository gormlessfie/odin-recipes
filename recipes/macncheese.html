Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and Cheese is a classic dish. It is considered as a dish that is too simple to screw it up! However, as you look more intricately into the steps of making each part of the dish, the cheese and the macaroni, it becomes apparent that it is quite complex in nature.

This dish while it consists of only two parts, there are a myriad of methods available online, all different. This goes to show that while this dish is only made up of two parts, there can be an limitless number of ingredients and combinations to make your own unique macaroni and cheese recipe. In this recipe, we will not be making a roux, but will be using the natural thickener present in macaroni. Furthermore, the cheese sauce is simplified to only two ingredients, with more being optional.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of evaporated milk
  • 500g Shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1000g Any pasta type
  • Optional: Smoked paprika, Cayenne

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a rolling boil.
  2. Once boiling, cook pasta until al dente (~8 minutes).
  3. Drain the pasta while keeping 450ml of pasta water.
  4. Return the pasta to the pot and add the evaporated milk.
  5. Turn off the heat and add the shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. Add pasta water to thicken the cheese mixture if desired.
